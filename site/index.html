A Layered Conversation

The discourse on Western hegemony is not a simple debate but a complex interplay of ideas about power, history, and ideology. While policy-oriented views focus on managing the current world order, academic and critical voices provide deep historical and theoretical insights into its origins, its maintenance, and the profound challenges it faces today. Understanding these different layers is crucial to grasping the intricate nature of global power dynamics.
